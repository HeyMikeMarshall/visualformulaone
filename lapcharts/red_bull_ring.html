Austrian Grand Prix
(Red Bull Ring)

The Red Bull Ring is a classic Formula One circuit reborn. Orignially known as the Österreichring, this circuit held Grand Prix events in the 70's, 80's, 90's and early 2000's. Ultimately falling into disuse, it was purchased and renovated by Dietrich Mateschitz, the billionaire co-founder of Red Bull. And it was reborn as the Red Bull Ring. Races on the circuit's current layout have been occurring only as recently as 2014.


Red Bull Ring, a modern track

This is a newly renovated track, and F1 has only been held here a few times in the modern technical regulations. This being the case, you can see a steady anual drop in lap times as the technology continues to develop, and as teams and drivers both build knowledge of the track.
