Grand Prix du Canada
(Circuit Gilles Villeneuve)

Grand Prix du Canada, or the Canadian Grand Prix is held at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal, Quebec. The circuit is located on an artificial island in the middle of the Saint Lawrence river.


Rain, red flags, and a '2-hour lap'

Lap times around the circuit have remained mostly steady over the years, with some minor fluctuation due to technical regulation changes and normal incidence of bad weather. Until the 2011 season, in which a torrential rain storm created a great deal of chaos on track until it forced a red flag on the race.
